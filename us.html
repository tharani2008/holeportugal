About Us

Learn more about Hole Golf Resort, the premier golf destination in Portugal

Our Story

Hole Golf Resort was created to offer a premier golfing experience in Portugal. Nestled in the heart of Portugal's stunning landscapes, our resort combines world-class golf courses with luxurious accommodations, making it a paradise for golf enthusiasts.

From our meticulously designed greens to our exceptional hospitality, we are committed to creating unforgettable experiences for our guests. Whether it's a challenging round of golf, serene relaxation, or discovering Portugal's rich heritage, we have something special for everyone.

Join us to create cherished memories in Portugal's breathtaking and diverse settings.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional service and facilities, providing memorable experiences for our guests while honoring Portugal's natural beauty and advancing the sport of golf.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be Portugal's premier destination for golf enthusiasts, offering world-class facilities, unparalleled service, and a uniquely Portuguese experience. We aspire to be a retreat where golf lovers can immerse themselves in both the sport and Portugal's captivating charm.
